My Top 5 series

Star Trek: Discovery

Best sci-fi series that covers futuristic elements with past and present realities to immerse you in a world full of endless possibilities.

Billions

Best Drama series showing you what is possible and how to be the best version of yourself.

Westworld

A thriller sci-fi with high level of artificial intelligence that will equally captivate you and at thesame time send of chill down your spine.

Jack Ryan

An action adventure thriller set in the early days of the chinese settling in the United States trying to have a better future but with alot of hate and resentment surrounding them.

Veep

A comedy drama set to distort the realities of modern day politics and giving you a fresh look on what politics can be.
